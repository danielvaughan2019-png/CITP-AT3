Proposal Summary

The project aims to address the ongoing energy challenge due to inconsistent funding and limited infrastructure through a new smart electric system integrated alongside existing infrastructure.

Overview of Design

The smart hybrid system achieves reliable, scalable energy through smart energy forecasting. This implementation is proposed to be supported through ARENA energy grants.

The design includes the following physical components:

  • Monocrystalline panels
  • LiFePO₄ battery array
  • Bypass switch
  • Inverter
  • MPPT charge controller
  • DC bus / combiner

The Lama Lama community will use this system every day for energy to support their lifestyle and cultural values.
