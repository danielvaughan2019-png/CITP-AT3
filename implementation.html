Section 8

Implementation Plan

How the project would be put into practice — construction, rollout, and long-term stewardship.

This section provides an understanding of how to implement the project in practice. It must be clear how the community is involved at every stage — from construction through to long-term ownership.

  • Stage 1 — Construction & Community Training

    How will the solution be constructed? Who will build it — the community, external contractors, or a combination? What training is required for community members to participate in construction and to use the solution day-to-day? [Approx. 2–4 sentences.]

  • Stage 2 — Introduction to Client & Community

    How will the solution be introduced to the client or community? What does the rollout process look like in the short term? How will uptake be encouraged and initial barriers addressed? [Approx. 2–4 sentences.]

  • Stage 3 — Long-term Use, Management & Maintenance

    How will the solution be managed and maintained over time? Who is responsible for ongoing maintenance? What resources — financial, human, or material — are required? What is the expected service life of the design? [Approx. 2–4 sentences.]

  • Stage 4 — Evaluation

    How will the design be evaluated once implemented? What indicators of success will be tracked? Who conducts the evaluation, and over what time frame? [2–4 sentences.]

  • Stage 5 — Community Ownership

    How can the client or community take full ownership of the project over time? What handover process is envisaged? How does the design reduce dependency on external expertise or funding? [2–4 sentences.]

  • Stage 6 — Repair & Contingency

    How will the solution be repaired if something goes wrong? Are replacement parts locally available? What contingency plans exist for common failure modes? [2–3 sentences.]

Future Impact on the Community

How will this project affect the future workings of the client or community? What changes — social, economic, environmental, or operational — might result from long-term implementation? Are there any potential unintended consequences, and how might they be mitigated? [Approx. 1 paragraph.]
