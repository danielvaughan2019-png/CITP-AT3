Section 7

Prototyping

Showing what the design looks like and testing whether it works.

Prototypes serve two purposes: to show what the design looks like, and to test whether the design — or an aspect of it — will work. Each team is expected to produce at least one prototype. Much of this section should be figures, tables, or photos.

What Was Prototyped & Why

Explain which aspect(s) of the design you chose to prototype and the reasoning behind this choice. What did you most need to test or demonstrate? Why was this the highest priority? [2–4 sentences.]

Construction Process

Describe step-by-step how the prototype was constructed. What materials were used? What tools were required? How long did it take? What challenges were encountered during construction, and how were they resolved? [Approx. 1 paragraph.]
